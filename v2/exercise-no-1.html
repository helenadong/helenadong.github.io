Exercise No.1

Fashion.  Film (2017)

Rhythmic and clinical, Exercise No.1 is an action sequence shaped by a selection of 27 anecdotes on various topics of ‘fashion’.

This short film premiered in 2018 at ASVOFF 10 in Paris, and was nominated for the ASVOFF Student Film Award.
