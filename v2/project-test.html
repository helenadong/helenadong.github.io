Vogue US / Vogue x Ms. Iyaaa

AR   .   Beauty   .   Fashion  (2020)

Ms. Iyaaa x Vogue was American Vogue’s first AR enhancement for an editorial.

This custom AR beauty effect was commissioned for Vogue’s September Issue to accompany the print editorial ‘To the Max’, as well as an online article which profiled the debut of the Byredo x Isamaya Ffrench makeup collection. Published on Vogue.com and subsequently shared on Instagram by @voguemagazine and @voguebeauty.
