I’m a designer & creative technologist working with fashion and interactive media.

My multidisciplinary practice provides design services to brands and individuals across fashion, beauty, lifestyle and publishing that seek to incite audience curiosity and impart visual narratives, primarily through digital interactions. At the core of my practice, there is an emphasis on transformation and functionality with the intention to evoke active modes of participation.

Encompassing Augmented Reality experiences, speculative fashion design and 3D image-making, this site documents my continuously evolving body of work, offering a reflection of our reality and glimpses into our potential future.

I also partake in research for the online resource New Reader.
