About

I am a designer and creative technologist assembling ideas through fashion, art and interactive media.

With an emphasis on transformation and utility, my practice seeks to engage audiences through custom experiences that prompt curiosity. Updating traditional avenues of design using 3D and Augmented Reality to offer a reflection of the present with a glimpse into an imaginary future.

I am also the researcher for the digital repository New Reader.

New York / Melbourne


Services

I specialise in the production of AR filters, 3D compositing, product renders and mixed-media artworks for both commercial and editorial projects.
