Scissors, Calculator And A Beige Coat

Fashion.  Film.  Game (2018)

Scissors, Calculator and a Beige Coat is a collection of garments, a performance piece and a text-based game.

Influenced by writings on Relational Aesthetics, spectatorship and contemporary performance practices, SCBC aims to induce contemplative engagement between the viewer, designer, performer and garment within the context of a fashion presentation.

One facet of SCBC takes the form of a choose-your-own-adventure story. Alluding to themes of independence and vulnerability, much of the narrative is based on personal reflections from my childhood, following my immigration to Australia with my mother in 2007.

You — as the player — make decisions that are in turn reflected in the garments through structural transformations and choreographed gestures, establishing a direct relationship between your actions and the visual outcomes of the collection. There are a total of 62 endings.

Concept, garments, choreography, video editing and digital development / Helena Dong
