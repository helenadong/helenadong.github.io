Zitsticka / Patch Dream

AR.  Beauty (2021)

An AR experience developed for ZitSticka, directly playing with both the function and look of the skincare brand’s iconic KILLA patch—a zit patch powered by microdart technology. Tap to place the KILLA patches on your face!
