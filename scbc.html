Scissors, Calculator And A Beige Coat

Fashion.  Film.  Game (2018)

Scissors, Calculator and a Beige Coat is a collection of garments, a performance piece and a text-based game.

Influenced by writings on Relational Aesthetics, spectatorship and contemporary performance practices, SCBC aims to induce contemplative engagement between the viewer, designer, performer and garment within the context of a fashion presentation.

One facet of SCBC takes the form of a choose-your-own-adventure story, alluding to themes of independence and vulnerability that predominantly stem from personal reflections on my childhood, following my immigration to Australia with my mother in 2007.

You——the protagonist——make narrative decisions that are subsequently portrayed in the garments through structural transformations and choreographed gestures, with the purpose of estabilshing a direct relationship between your chosen path and the visual outcome of the collection. There are 62 paths in total.
