Optical Volume

Eyewear.  Publication (2022)

Optical Volume is the first book from the Network Officium series. It is part of a collection of speculative proposals that pertain to wearable technologies, and how branding, material and technical elements can be combined for use in an imaginary capacity.

In this issue, we use 3D rener and illustration to hypothesize a playful collection of "smart-eyewear" while featuring a selection of works from a global network of contemporary artists as key visual references.
