Optical Volume

Eyewear.  Publication (2022)

Optical Volume is a collection of ten proposals pertaining to wearable technologies that observe how branding, material and technical elements could be combined for use in a speculative capacity.

Throughout this book, 3D renders and illustrations were used to hypothesize a series of smart glasses that branch into various technologies——including AI/ML, material engineering and extended reality——while featuring a selection of works from a global network of contemporary artists and designers as key visual references.

▊

Research & Editing / Helena Dong, Max Saward, Ari Neilsson
Curation & Book Design / Max Saward
Eyewear Design & 3D / Helena Dong
Text / Ari Neilsson
Given the nature of speculative design, the concepts presented in this volume may not seek to suggest tangible solutions so much as they incite conversations about a set of circumstances. Though many of the designs do address themselves to a greater ecosystem of issues ranging from environmental concerns to a desire for sensory escapism, their containment to the field of eyewear also insists that the discussions they instigate be held on their own playful terms.
