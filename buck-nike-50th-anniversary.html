NIKE / 50TH ANNIVERSARY

AR.  BUCK (2022)

A global retail WebAR activation in celebration of Nike's 50th anniversary, which I had the pleasure of taking part as associate creative director through BUCK.

For this project, we partnered with the Nike Global Brand Experience team to bring the brand's past, present, and future to life in stores around the world through a WebAR experience that captures the spirit of DNA (Department of Nike Archives) and supports the future of storytelling within the retail space.
