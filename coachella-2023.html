COACHELLA / HOLOFLUX'S RIFT

AR.  Space (2023)

Holoflux's Rift is an AR extension of the physical installation, Holoflux.

Conceived by cyberphysical architect Güvenç Özel for Coachella 2023, Holoflux was a multifaceted installation that served as a landmark for the festival. My contribution to this venture was to extend the physical structure into the digital space, through a collaboration with Coachella and Meta.

Holoflux's Rift was meticulously designed to mirror the temporal rhythm of the physical installation, shifting throughout the day. Starting with an opalescent base, it morphed into luminous wireframes by evening, a transformation that dissolved the chrome metaballs and instigated a flux of color-changing particles. These particles, synchronized with the evening projections, enveloped the physical structure to create a visually immersive experience.

Concept / Ozel Office
AR Development / Helena Dong
Production Support / Meta Spark
The AR extension cycled through three distinct modes: day, sunset, and evening. Each mode offered variations in form, color, and animation that refreshed with each new day, establishing a continually evolving spectacle.

To facilitate a cohesive, site-specific experience, Holoflux's Rift utilized a combination of target-tracking and multiplane-tracking capabilities. This ensured a unified integration of the physical and virtual components.
