About

Helena Dong is a designer and creative technologist based in NYC, interweaving conceptual undercurrents from fashion and Extended Reality. Her practice seeks to prompt curiosity through customized interactions, and envisages the possibilities of the future while reflecting on the present. Besides dreaming of speculative wearables, she designs, develops, and creative directs XR experiences.

Helena is currently an art director at BUCK, where she collaborates on experiential projects with a focus on emergent technologies.
