About

Helena Dong is an experiential art director and creative technologist based in New York.

She designs transient activations across AR/MR/VR and envisions systems that don't yet exist, all with the intention of inviting curiosity and fostering thoughtful participation. Balancing precision and play, her work reinterprets the material world—whether it's a space, a body, or a face.

A dreamer at heart, Helena is enthralled by themes of space-time, movement, and the esoteric. You could even ask her to read tarot for you.

Currently, Helena is a senior art director at BUCK.
