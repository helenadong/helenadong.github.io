About

Helena Dong is a designer and creative technologist based in NYC, integrating conceptual undercurrents from fashion and Augmented Reality. Her practice seeks to prompt curiosity through customized experiences, and envisages the possibilities of the future while reflecting on the present.

Helena is also an Art Director at BUCK, where she collaborates on experiential projects with a focus on emergent technologies.
