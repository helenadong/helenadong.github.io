About

Helena Dong is a designer and creative technologist based in NYC, interweaving conceptual undercurrents from fashion, performance and augmented reality. Central to her multidisciplinary practice is the speculation of future possibilities while distilling curiosity through customized interactions.

Since engaging with creative tech in 2020, Helena has designed, developed and directed AR experiences for a multitude of distinguished brands and organizations.

Helena is currently an art director at BUCK, where she collaborates on experiential projects driven by immersive technologies.
