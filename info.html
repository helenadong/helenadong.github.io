About

Helena Dong is an interdisciplinary designer and creative technologist based in NYC, integrating conceptual undercurrents from fashion and XR industries. Her practice seeks to prompt curiosity through customized experiences, and envisages the possibilities of the future while meditating on the present.


Services

Specializing in the design and development of social AR experiences, 3D compositing and mixed-media artworks.
