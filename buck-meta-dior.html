META X DIOR / THE ATELIER OF DREAMS

AR.  Beauty.  Fashion (2022)

The Atelier of Dreams AR campaign emerged as a collaboration between Dior, BUCK, and Meta, where the celestial motifs in the campaign illustrations, by Italian artist Pietro Ruffo, were transformed into a series of immersive AR experiences, each imbued with a distinct sense of vitality and spatial presence.

This endeavor resulted an elegant and sumptuous interplay between the physical and the virtual, manifested through four site-specific installations in Paris and Seoul, an at-home activation, and a point-of-sale experience spanning hundreds of Dior's retail locations worldwide.
