COACHELLA / THIRD CONTACT IN NYC

AR (2022)

In 2022, I had the pleasure of creating a geo-anchored AR experience for the virtual world of Coachella——the Coachellaverse. As a global collaboration, Third Contact in NYC is one of five immersive, location-based AR effects for this year's campaign.

The nature of geo-anchoring is that the experience reveals itself only when you're physically present at one of the designated landmarks: Times Square, Central Park and Little Island. This sense of persistence dynamically positions the experience in the context of its environment, and aims to reinforce our appreciation for tangible space through the instilment of serenity.
