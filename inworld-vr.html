InWorld / VR World-Building Prototype

Space.  VR (2021)

InWorld is a VR world-building prototype application, developed in Unity and demoed in Meta Quest. This project conceived as part of the XR Terra AR/VR Design and Development Bootcamps, and sought to generate a set of tools that would facilitate a virtual space for collaborative learning and social events.
