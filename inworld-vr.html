In World / VR World-Building Prototype

Space.  VR (2021)

In World is a VR world-building prototype application developed in Unity, for the Oculus Quest. Our final group project part of the XR Terra AR/VR Design Bootcamp.

The use of social VR environments for events, learning, networking and coworking has been a jump as more individuals have had the ability to work remotely and the platforms for these environments are growing. Altspace, Facebook Horizon, VR Chat, Engage…These platforms offer quality of environmental design, the ability to rapidly reformat learning environments to fit user needs, and implement working solutions.

The basis of this project is to develop and design an application prototype on environment building for virtual collaborative learning environments. The goal of this project is to create a set of generative tools to assist with the design of these environments in a virtual setting. Each team will be made up of members from the XR Terra design and development bootcamps, and will work collaboratively together to come up with a prototype solution.

A space for community and collaboration which hosts spotlight areas, stage areas. The environment should feel natural. Wide open spaces formats are encouraged. Ability to change skybox

A space for self study, learning and collaboration which hosts group learning spaces, social environments and smaller areas for self study.

Project was built as an .apk for the Oculus Quest.
Regulation of environment design, instilling a sense of consistency that makes the environments feel welcoming and less chaotic. A balanced approach that allows users to tweak the visual design system within reason, whilst still maintaining structure and rules. Curated inventory of objects within the space to match the aesthetic of the environment.
